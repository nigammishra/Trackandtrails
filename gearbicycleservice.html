Gear Bicycle Service Package

Gear Bicycle Repair & Service at Fix My Cycle!Conquer Shifting Issues

Is your gear cycle giving you trouble? Experiencing clunky shifting, grinding noises, or complete gear failure? Fix My Cycle offers comprehensive gear bicycle repair and service packages to get your ride back in top form!

We specialize in all things gears:

  • Gear Adjustments & Tuning: We can diagnose and fix common gear problems like inaccurate shifting, chain slippage, and misalignment.
  • Derailleur Service: Keep your derailleur operating smoothly with adjustments, cleaning, and lubrication.
  • Cassette & Chainring Inspection: We’ll identify worn-out components before they cause further issues.
  • Complete Drivetrain Maintenance: Our services ensure a clean, efficient, and quiet drivetrain for optimal performance.

Choose the service that fits your needs:

Standard Service (Perfect for Regular Maintenance):

  • Address minor gear adjustments and shifting issues.
  • Clean and degrease your drivetrain for a smoother ride.
  • Ensure confident stopping power with brake adjustments.

Advanced Service (Ideal for a More In-Depth Tune-Up):

Everything in the Standard Service, Plus:

  • Comprehensive gear adjustments for optimized performance.
  • Derailleur hanger alignment check and adjustments (if needed).
  • Inspection for wear and tear on cassette and chainrings.
  • Complete lubrication of your entire drivetrain.
