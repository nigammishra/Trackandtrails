Our Services

DOOR STEP SERVICE

FixMyCycle company offers doorstep bicycle services that provide customers with convenience and flexibility when it comes to maintaining their bikes. These services allow customers to schedule appointments at times that fit their busy schedules, eliminating the need to travel to a physical location or wait for a technician to arrive. The technicians come directly to the customer’s home or office, fully equipped with all the necessary tools and parts to repair or maintain the bicycle.

IN-STORE SERVICES

Prefer to bring your bike into our state-of-the-art facility? No problem! Our knowledgeable staff will treat your ride like royalty, utilizing only top-quality components and equipment to ensure a flawless finish every time.

PICK UP AND DROP SERVICES

Hassle-free bike repairs at your convenience! Book online or via our website, and our friendly techs will collect your bike from any location before bringing it to our shop for expert care. We’ll then safely return your bike to its original spot – quick, easy, and flexible!

APARTMENT SERVICE CAMPS

We offer assistance in repairing or maintaining your bicycle, our on-site maintenance team has got you covered! We offer comprehensive bicycle repair packages for apartment communities. Starting from basic tune-ups to full overhauls using high-quality parts. You can request an appointment via our website or contact us directly by phone or email.

CORPORATE SERVICE CAMPS

We provide bicycle maintenance services specifically tailored for corporate companies that use bicycles as a mode of transportation within their campuses or facilities. This not only helps keep their employees healthy but also reduces carbon footprint and traffic congestion.

EVENT SUPPORT

Fix my Cycle in partnership with local organizations, proudly supports various cycling events throughout the year. We understand that these events not only encourage physical activity but also foster community engagement and environmental awareness. As such, we strive to ensure every participant has an enjoyable experience during their ride. Prior to each event, we work closely with organizers to identify their needs and tailor our support accordingly. This includes supplying high-quality bikes, helmets, spare parts, tools, and repair kits, ensuring everything is in excellent condition for use on race day. We remain committed to providing exceptional support services that help make these events successful and memorable experiences for everyone involved!

PRE-OWNED CYCLES

Discover effortless buying and selling of genuine pre-owned bikes with FMC Assured, FixMyCycle’s dedicated platform. Our expert-certified bicycles offer you unparalleled quality and confidence while making your transaction a breeze. We’ve taken the hassle out of purchasing second-hand bikes by providing transparent pricing, comprehensive inspection reports, and trusted warranties.
