How it all Begin

For the past eight incredible years, we’ve been your go-to destination for everything cycling. From your first bike ride to your latest adventure, we’ve been here to support every mile of your journey. Our store isn't just about selling bikes—it's about fostering a community of passionate riders, offering expert advice, and ensuring that every ride is smooth and enjoyable. We're not just a bike shop; we're your cycling family.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Whether you’re a seasoned cyclist or just getting started, we’re committed to providing you with top-notch service, a fantastic selection of bikes, and all the gear you need to keep rolling.

Here’s to many more years of cycling, adventures, and community. Ride on with us! Visit us today and let’s celebrate together!

Our Mission

Our vision is to provide the perfect bike for every rider while becoming Bhubaneswar's leading provider of efficient, eco-friendly, and convenient door-to-door bicycle services. By leveraging technology, innovative business models, and strategic partnerships, we aim to enhance our customers' quality of life while significantly reducing carbon emissions. Together, let’s promote a greener, healthier future!
