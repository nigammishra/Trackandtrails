Dismantling and Boxing Package

Stress-Free Bike Storage & Transport

Life takes you places, and sometimes that means storing your bike for a while. At Riders point, we understand the importance of keeping your cherished cycles safe! Our exclusive “Dismantle & Box” service package is designed to help you efficiently pack away your bike, whether it’s a road bike, mountain bike, or another type, for storage or transport.

Our expert technicians will meticulously disassemble your bike, ensuring all parts are carefully labeled and stored for a smooth reassembly process later. We’ll then place the components back into the original manufacturer’s boxes (if available) or use appropriate packing materials to minimize the risk of damage during transport.

Benefits of Riders point’s Dismantle & Box Service:

  • Effortless Storage: Pack your bike away in even confined spaces.
  • Safe Transportation: Minimize the risk of damage during transport.
    Hassle-Free Reassembly: Easily reassemble your bike when you’re ready to ride again.

Schedule your Dismantle & Box service today and experience the Riders point difference!
